Test Labs Overview

Embark on a puzzling journey as an enigmatic ball trapped in a mysterious test lab. Stranded and uncertain of your origin, your only guide is the voice directing you through a series of mind-bending experiments.

Gameplay Overview:

  • Master the Dash: Gain the power to dash through obstacles, adding a dynamic layer to your puzzle-solving skills.
  • Intriguing Puzzles: Navigate through perplexing puzzles involving moving boxes, unlocking doors, and even breaking down walls.
  • Immersive Test Lab Environment: Explore a visually captivating test lab filled with challenges, each designed to test your wit and agility.
  • Multiple Test Labs: Progress through three distinct test labs, each offering a unique set of challenges and puzzles.

Test Labs Breakdown:

  • Test Lab 1: Navigate through 4 levels of increasing complexity.
  • Test Lab 2: Tackle 8 levels that push your skills to the limit.
  • Test Lab 3: Experience a culmination of challenges with 1 standalone level followed by an intricate combination of 5 levels.

Why Play?

Challenge yourself with unique puzzles, harness newfound abilities, and unravel the mysteries of the test lab. Will you escape the confines of experimentation, or is there more to your journey than meets the eye?

Dive into Test Labs now and navigate the twists and turns of this captivating puzzle adventure!
